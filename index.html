Why South Africa?

South Africa also called the World in one country. A place where you can watch the sunrise from the top of a mountain catch a morning wave in the ocean and spot an elephant roaming at the dusk all in the same day.

There is a reason why former President Nelson Mandela proclaimed South Africa as the Rainbow Nation – the country is diverse and colorful in every way.

Once you have reach down in South Africa you’ll be fully immersed in the music and lifestyle of a blend of welcoming people. Nevertheless English is widely spoken by all groups which makes travel and living a Breeze

South Africa is known for its scenery and wildlife. No matter which part of the country you travel to you will find diverse and mesmerizing landscapes and wildlife.

5 Reasons to do your voluntary work at our Project in South Africa

  1. Gain confidence: You will grow in your independence and self reliance. Get to now yourself better and what you urge from life and your future.
  2. Learn English: This ist the perfect opportunity to improve your English by speaking, hearing and writing.
  3. Good Weather and great Opportunities: Our weather invites you to enjoy the outdoors and take advantage of nearly 3000km coastline
  4. Natural Beauty: South Africa’s scenic wonders are legendary From table Mountain to God’s Window our mountains forests coasts and deserts will feast your eye and lift your spirit..
  5. Rainbow Nation: South Africa celebrates all African and immigrant cultures. We have 11 official languages and many more to teach.
